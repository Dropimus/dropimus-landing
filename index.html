The Protocol Stack

Three Layers.
One Credential.

Proof of Fade governs how calls are anchored and settled. Proof of Foresight is the credential that emerges. Dropimus is the platform where both live.

Anchor Any Call

Connect your wallet. State your call on anything — airdrops, prices, elections, DeFi, sports. Set a resolution date. Submit. Timestamped on-chain. Immutable. Permanent from that moment forward.

Stake Honor & Capital

Honor is non-purchasable reputation earned exclusively through accuracy over time. Capital is financial skin in the game. Both are at stake on every call you make. Both matter at resolution.

Evidence on Both Sides

Your call stands by default. Anyone — supporters or challengers — can submit evidence weighted by proof quality. If the weight tips against your call, it gets FADED. If nobody disputes, it stands.

Adversarial Resolution

On-chain events auto-resolve. Everything else resolves through evidence from both sides. Honor-weighted consensus evaluates all submissions and determines the final outcome.

PROVEN or FADED

PROVEN — Honor increases, capital settled in your favor, credential updated. FADED — Honor decreases, capital lost, recorded permanently. Nothing deleted. Nothing hidden. Both outcomes are honest.

Soulbound Credential

Your Proof of Foresight credential is portable, permanent, and non-transferable. Built on-chain as a soulbound NFT (ERC-5114 on Base). Earned through accuracy alone — never purchasable, never stolen.

Proof Multipliers

Research Quality
= Influence Weight

Not all calls carry equal weight. The protocol multiplies your influence based on what you attach as proof. Noise is nearly worthless. Genuine insight compounds over time.

Opinion Only
0.02×
Pure opinion with no supporting evidence. Nearly zero influence. You can make the call — the protocol almost ignores it at resolution.
Soft Proof
2×
Tokenomics analysis, social sentiment, market structure. A credible starting point — but not enough to lead consensus against hard data.
Hard On-Chain Data
6×
Security audits, verified on-chain data, doxxed teams. Maximum influence. This separates serious researchers from the noise they drown out.

Everything you need to know about Dropimus and the Proof of Foresight protocol.

Dropimus is a Proof of Foresight protocol — a decentralized system where anyone can anchor a call on anything before the outcome is known, stake reputation and capital on it, and build a permanent verifiable track record of genuine foresight on-chain. Your calls are timestamped before outcomes exist, proven by Proof of Fade, and yours forever.
Prediction markets need counterparties — Dropimus needs only you. Prediction markets require platform-created markets — Dropimus is permissionless. Prediction markets measure trading skill — Dropimus measures foresight quality. Prediction markets show P&L — Dropimus shows a track record of being right before everyone else. And prediction markets resolve by oracle or vote — Dropimus resolves through evidence from both sides, with Honor-weighted consensus determining PROVEN or FADED.
Anything with an outcome that resolves in reality. Crypto airdrops, token prices, protocol events, L1/L2 developments, elections, policy outcomes, currency movements, sports results, tournament predictions, DeFi governance votes, TVL movements — if it has a future outcome, you can anchor a call on it.
These are the two final states for every call on Dropimus:
PROVEN — your call survived. Honor increases, capital settled in your favor, Proof of Foresight credential updated.FADED — enough evidence was submitted against your call. Honor decreases, capital lost. Recorded permanently.

Both outcomes are permanent. FADED calls are not deleted — an honest record of misses is part of what makes a record of hits meaningful.
Honor is the reputation layer running parallel to financial stakes. It cannot be purchased, transferred, or borrowed. It is earned exclusively through accuracy over time — verified research, correct calls, and quality evidence that survives challenge. Proof multipliers determine how much influence your Honor carries: opinion alone is 0.02×, soft proof is 2×, hard on-chain data is 6×.
Honor is non-transferable by design. A regular token could be sold — which would let people buy reputation they never earned, breaking the entire system. As a soulbound NFT (ERC-5114 on Base), Honor is permanently tied to the wallet that did the work. It can grow with accuracy, decay with inactivity, and be slashed for wrong calls — but it can never be moved or sold.
Your call stands by default. Anyone — whether they support or want to challenge the call — can submit evidence. Supporters submit proof that strengthens the call. Challengers submit proof against it. All evidence is weighted by proof quality — hard on-chain data carries 6× the influence of pure opinion. Honor-weighted consensus evaluates everything. If the weight tips against the call, it gets FADED. On-chain events auto-resolve without this process.
