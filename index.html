Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Dropimus, airdrops, and the Proof of Fade protocol.

Dropimus is a proof layer for crypto airdrops. Instead of relying on hype or opinion, it lets users stake earned reputation — called Honor — to make verifiable predictions on whether airdrops will deliver value. Accuracy builds influence over time. Wrong calls reduce your stake. The result is a signal layer where research quality determines weight, not loudness.
No crypto background needed. They're just colourful names for Yes and No:
LAMBO = Yes (bullish — the airdrop will pay off) DUST = No (bearish — the airdrop will disappoint)

When you make a Call on Dropimus you're simply predicting whether a specific airdrop will deliver significant value (LAMBO / Yes) or be largely worthless (DUST / No). The names are crypto slang — the mechanic is straightforward prediction backed by earned reputation.
A crypto airdrop is when a blockchain project distributes free tokens to wallets that meet certain criteria — usually users who participated in the protocol early or completed specific tasks. Airdrops transferred over $4.5 billion in 2025 alone. The problem is that most people don't know which airdrops will be valuable before they happen. Dropimus creates a reputation layer so that well-researched predictions rise above noise.
Honor is the reputation currency on Dropimus. It cannot be purchased — it must be earned through verified work: labeling grouped on-chain wallet actions, creating original playbooks, having your playbooks forked by other users, and having your playbook linked to an official airdrop submission. You need at least 20 Honor before you can make Calls. This keeps the signal layer filled with people who have done real research, not just opinions.
A Playbook is a structured guide that documents how to participate in a specific airdrop — the tasks, steps, and requirements involved. Think of it like a recipe for airdrop participation. When other users fork (copy and customise) your Playbook, or when it gets officially linked to an airdrop submission, you earn Honor as the creator. Playbooks are verifiable proof of research quality.
Wrong Calls reduce your staked Honor. This is intentional — it makes every prediction a real commitment backed by reputation, not a costless opinion. Repeated wrong Calls compound losses. Consistent accuracy compounds gains. Inactivity triggers a slow decay. The protocol rewards research and penalises noise.
Proof of Fade weights each Call by the quality of research attached to it. No proof carries just 0.02× influence. Soft proof (market analysis, sentiment) carries 2×. Hard proof (audits, verified on-chain data, doxxing) carries up to 6×. Consensus resolves automatically when thresholds are met, with a 24-hour dispute window before settlement. Winners gain Honor; incorrect callers lose stake.
There are three tiers: Initiate (free) gives you 3 original guides, limited daily labels, and a stake cap of 300. Operator ($19/month) expands to 30 guides, 50 editable forks, full analytics, and a 5,000 stake cap. Apex ($79/month) removes all limits. Upgrades expand scope and throughput — they do not create reputation. Honor is earned on the record regardless of plan. Payments are processed in crypto via NOWPayments.
