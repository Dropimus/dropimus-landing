Earned Influence

01

Call It — LAMBO or DUST

Stake Honor to predict whether an airdrop will pay off (LAMBO = Yes) or disappoint (DUST = No). No stake means no skin in the game.

02

Back It with Proof

Attach verifiable research to your Call. Labels, playbooks, and on-chain data each carry multiplied influence weight versus bare opinion.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Dropimus, airdrops, and the Proof of Fade protocol.

Dropimus is a proof layer for crypto airdrops. Instead of hype or opinion, it lets users stake earned reputation — called Honor — to make verifiable predictions on whether airdrops will deliver value. Accuracy builds influence. Wrong calls reduce your stake. Research quality determines weight, not loudness.
No crypto background needed. They're simply creative names for Yes and No:
LAMBO = Yes — the airdrop will pay off DUST = No — the airdrop will disappoint

The names are crypto slang. The mechanic is straightforward: predict an outcome, back it with research, get scored on accuracy.
Honor is the reputation currency on Dropimus. It cannot be purchased — it must be earned through verified work: labeling grouped on-chain wallet actions, creating original playbooks, having your playbooks forked, and having a playbook linked to an official airdrop submission. You need at least 20 Honor before making Calls.
Honor is non-transferable by design. A regular token could be sold, which would let people buy reputation they never earned — breaking the entire system. As a soulbound NFT (ERC-5114 on Base), Honor is permanently tied to the wallet that did the work. It can grow with accuracy, decay with inactivity, and be slashed for wrong calls — but it can never be moved or sold. Your reputation stays yours, and only yours.
A Playbook is a structured guide documenting how to participate in a specific airdrop — tasks, steps, and requirements. Think of it as a recipe. When others fork (copy and customise) your Playbook, or when it gets linked to an official submission, you earn Honor as the creator.
Wrong Calls reduce your staked Honor. This is intentional — every prediction is a real commitment backed by reputation, not a free opinion. Repeated wrong Calls compound losses. Consistent accuracy compounds gains. Inactivity triggers slow decay. The protocol rewards research and penalises noise.
Each Call is weighted by research quality: no proof = 0.02×, soft proof (analysis, sentiment) = 2×, hard proof (audits, verified on-chain data) = 6×. Consensus resolves automatically when thresholds are met, with a 24-hour dispute window. Winners gain Honor; wrong callers lose stake.
